Cluely (cluely.com) is the visual benchmark. Directly observed on 2026-06-08 via Playwright at 1280px desktop and 390px mobile.

**Hero composition (directly observed):**
- Full-bleed hero with a painted/illustrated background — warm orange-to-blue gradient sky with stylized mountain silhouettes. This is NOT a photo or a canvas — it is a CSS/SVG illustrated scene that fills the entire viewport with color and depth.
- Hero headline is center-aligned, large, sans-serif, extremely high contrast white on the illustrated background. Bold, 3-word structure: "#1 Undetectable AI for Meetings"
- Headline is backed by a dramatic visual — not just text floating on a dark bg.
- Single primary CTA ("Get for Mac") is a solid blue pill button, center-aligned, placed directly under the sub-headline. No competing elements.
- NO secondary text clutter around the CTA. It is singular and clear.

**Product UI presentation (directly observed):**
- The actual product UI — a MacBook window with a Zoom call open and the Cluely overlay panel visible — is the dominant visual below the fold, but still within the hero scroll area.
- The product window is rendered as a floating, 3D-perspective-tilted UI frame. It literally shows the product in context: two faces on a video call, with the Cluely AI sidebar visible.
- This is the core of what makes Cluely feel real and tangible: the product is CENTER STAGE. You see it before you read about it.
- The UI card is large — roughly 80% of viewport width — and has macOS window chrome (title bar, traffic lights).

**Depth and layering:**
- The product window floats over the illustrated background with a strong drop shadow, creating real z-axis depth.
- The mountain/sky background recedes behind the product window, giving a cinematic frame.
- There is a clear foreground (product window) > midground (text) > background (illustration) hierarchy.

**Typography:**
- Very large headline, short word count (5 words). No body copy above the fold except one sub-line.
- Font is clean, slightly rounded sans-serif. High confidence sizing — nothing feels tentative.
- Subline is small and restrained: "Cluely takes perfect meeting notes and gives real-time answers, all while completely undetectable."

**Navigation:**
- Ultra-minimal: logo left, 3 nav links right (Undetectability, Reviews, Blog), one CTA button. Nothing else.
- Nav bar has subtle background blur / semi-transparent treatment.

**Section structure below hero:**
- After the hero, sections use a light background (near-white / very light gray) — a dramatic contrast shift from the dark hero.
- This contrast makes the page feel like it has chapters, not a long scroll.
- FAQ section uses accordion pattern, minimal and clean.
- Closing CTA section repeats the core promise with a single action button.

**What makes it feel premium:**
1. The product is VISIBLE from the first second — you don't need to imagine what it looks like.
2. The background illustration is unique and distinctive — it's not a gradient or a photo, it's a scene.
3. The CTA is singular. There is no ambiguity about what to do.
4. Depth: layers of foreground/background create a physical sense of space.
5. Restraint: very few words above the fold. Every element earns its place.
6. Light-to-dark contrast between sections creates visual breathing room.

**Why:** This is the quality bar for all RepoMax visual design audits.

**How to apply:** When evaluating RepoMax, ask — does this section have the equivalent depth, product visibility, and CTA clarity that Cluely achieves? Use these specific observations, not vague "feels more premium" language.
